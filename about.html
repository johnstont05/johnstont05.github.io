My name is Taylor Johnston and I am a sophomore studying Journalism and Visual Communication at Ohio University. I am a news reporter and the Digital Production Editor for The Post, a student-run newspaper on campus. This summer I am an editorial intern at the Ohio Magazine in Cleveland, OH.

I have created this website to showcase my ultimate career goal. I would like to write my own stories while also making them visually appealing. Currently, I am a self-taught coder and I am looking to expand my skills in the upcoming years while I am still attending school along with perfecting my journalism skills in hopes to find my dream job where I can use both of my passions of design and writing everyday.

This site includes different works of mine ranging from my writing, web design skills and publication design skills.
